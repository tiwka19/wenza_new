Bearings Industry

Bearings are precision components that enable machinery to reduce friction and support rotating parts. They come in various shapes and sizes, including ball bearings, roller bearings, and plain bearings, each designed for specific applications. Bearings are manufactured with extreme precision and attention to detail, ensuring the highest quality and reliability.

To discover more about WENZA solutions for bearings manufacturing, you can refer to the article below. Or download our presentation dedicated to industry-specific solutions.

Ready-Made Solutions for Bearings Manufacturing

Precision is the cornerstone of bearings manufacturing. The process starts with the selection of the material, which is usually a high-grade steel, chosen for its durability and load-bearing capacity. To achieve the necessary tolerances, manufacturers use computer numerical control (CNC) machines and laser measuring systems. These methods allow for precise shaping and dimension control, ensuring the bearings' compatibility with various machinery and applications.
