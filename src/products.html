Production Process

We have a full cycle production equipped with up-to-date machinery. The nomenclature and size range fully comply with ISO 1832. Our company also produces non-standardized products according to customer drawings.

Each insert has its own identification number, which can be used to track when, by whom and how it was made, namely: if the insert was coated, ground, soldered, cut, when the composite was sintered, from what powder supplies, with what characteristics, and so on.

Tailored Technical Solutions

We know that there are cases when it is difficult to find the right combination of inserts and tooling from a wide range of cutting tools to solve the tasks of the production site in the best possible way. For this purpose, we have created a convenient form, by filling in which you will provide our specialists with thorough information about the workpiece machining at the production site.
